Ever feel like you're way too relaxed while browsing the internet? Do you sometimes wish someone was there to stare directly into your soul and judge your every click? Or maybe you just want a little desk buddy who silently judges your horrible taste in memes. 

Look no further. **Judging Eyes** is a Chrome Extension that injects a pair of fully interactive, judgemental (or chill, or sleepy!) eyes right onto your webpage. 

## ✨ Features

### 👁️ Core Mechanics
- **Stalker Mode:** The eyes fluently track your cursor around the page in real-time. They know exactly what you're hovering over.
- **Dynamic Fatigue System:** They start off alert, but if you keep browsing or leave them idle for too long, they'll start to droop and eventually fall asleep.

### 🌦️ Weather Reactions (New!)
The eyes check your local weather and react to the elements!
- **Hot:** The eyes will continuously sweat. Expose them to the heat for too long, and they'll get sunburned.
- **Cold:** They will visibly shiver and turn a frosty blue.
- **Rain & Snow:** Watch teardrops fall or the eyes freeze over depending on precipitation.
- **Storm:** The skies will flash with lightning strikes.

### 🎮 Interactive Physics
- **Drag & Drop:** Don't like where they're floating? Grab them! They'll dangle helplessly in the air while you drag them, and get annoyed when you drop them.
- **Poke to Annoy:** Click them to poke their eyes. They'll recoil and immediately run away to a new random spot on the screen.
- **Meme Rage:** Poke them 4 times in rapid succession and witness their ultimate fury (they turn into a wildly vibrating red meme face and blink out of existence to cool off).


### 🎭 Customization
Use the extension popup to customize their vibe:
- **Models:** Default, Anime, Cartoon, Drunken, or Psychopathic.
- **Moods:** Chill, Judgmental, Sleepy.
- **Size:** Adjust the scale of the eyes to fit your screen.

## 🚀 How to Install

1. Download or clone this repository to your computer.
2. Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Turn on the **Developer mode** toggle switch in the top right corner.
4. Click the **Load unpacked** button in the top left.
5. Select the repository folder containing these files. 
6. Pin the extension to your toolbar to easily change models and weather settings!

## 🛠️ Under The Hood

Built entirely with pure, vanilla **JavaScript** (Manifest V3), **HTML**, and **CSS**. 
All of the fluid recoil, drag physics, dragging constraints, weather styling, and meme animations are beautifully handled through custom CSS keyframes and vanilla `requestAnimationFrame` hooks. No heavy external frameworks were harmed in the making of this extension.
